Our History

Started in 1991 as a family business, the company initially supplied to 84 major tea garden companies. In 2001, the brand
underwent a transformation, partnering with renowned multinational companies like Escorts Construction, Greaves Cotton,
and Crompton Greaves. This ushered in a new era of silent gensets across North-East India. The company excelled in
manufacturing, marketing, and providing round-the-clock services for generators and construction equipment,
earning a loyal customer base.
