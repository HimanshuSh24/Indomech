Who We Are

WELCOME TO INCOMECH

We are a leading provider power-related products and expert consultants in Electrical and Mechanical Engineering. Our services now extend to Solar Power Solutions, Residential Real Estate Projects, and Industrial Park Construction.

Our central office, located in the heart of the city, is equipped with modern communication and technological facilities. Additionally, our state-of-the-art manufacturing unit at the Changsari Industrial Growth Centre, Guwahati, is equipped for assembling, storage, and product testing.

We actively participate in government tenders, both online and offline, and are registered with the PWD, Assam Electricity Board, and Indian Railways. As a licensed electrical contractor, we are committed to delivering high-quality solutions.
